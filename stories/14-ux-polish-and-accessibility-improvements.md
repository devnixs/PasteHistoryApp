# Story 14 - UX Polish and Accessibility Improvements

## Goal

Refine the picker and surrounding workflow so the app feels native, low-friction, and fully keyboard-centric.

## Why this matters

Utility apps live or die on perceived speed and polish more than raw feature count.

## Scope

* Improve picker positioning and dismissal behavior.
* Tighten focus behavior around picker open and close.
* Improve keyboard focus visibility and accessibility labels.
* Add concise user-facing feedback for fallback paste scenarios.
* Polish error messages and setup guidance.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Picker open and close behavior is predictable and does not leave focus in a broken state.
* Keyboard-only use remains complete across the primary workflow.
* Accessibility labels and focus states are present on major picker actions.
* Fallback paste guidance is concise and shown only when useful.
* UI copy around permissions and failures is short and clear.
